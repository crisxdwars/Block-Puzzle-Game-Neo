How to Play
Objective
Stack falling Blocks to complete full rows. Every time you clear a line, a quiz question appears. Answer correctly to keep playing — one wrong answer and it's Game Over.
Controls
← →Move left / right
↑Rotate piece
↓Soft drop
SpaceHard drop
Difficulty & Timer
Questions get harder as you answer more. Each difficulty has its own time limit:
DifficultyTriggers AfterTimer
● EASYStart of game - 2 correct answers30 seconds
● MEDIUM3 correct answers20 seconds
● HARD6 correct answers10 seconds
Scoring
1 line = 100 pts · 2 lines = 300 pts · 3 lines = 500 pts · 4 lines = 800 pts (×level). Correct quiz answers award a bonus of 50 points. Hard drops give 2 pts per row fallen.
